About Me

Hello! I'm Kristian Dimaranan, a passionate data analyst, business analyst, and visualization expert with over a decade of experience turning complex data into actionable insights. DataBrew is my portfolio, where I combine my love for data and coffee to showcase impactful Power BI dashboards and transformative solutions for businesses

Throughout my career, I’ve not only created data-driven insights but also led transformative projects that have reshaped business processes and improved operational efficiency:

As Head of Data Management & Analytics at Aldar, I partnered with senior stakeholders to design and deliver over 50 dashboards, aligning data solutions with business strategy and KPIs while streamlining reporting processes. As the Solution Architect for HCM systems, I spearheaded the transformation of HR systems by automating over 90% of manual processes, leading multi-million-dollar Oracle implementation projects, and utilizing analytics to drive workforce improvement programs As the IT Global Service Operations Section Head at Onsemi, I led a team of 11 to manage IT operations across manufacturing sites in APAC, the US, and Europe. I established governance frameworks, enhanced service delivery through Lean Six Sigma methodologies, and ensured 99.89% system uptime, supporting critical manufacturing operations globally

I specialize in bridging the gap between technical solutions and business needs—translating complex requirements into scalable, automated systems that drive efficiency, improve user adoption, and deliver meaningful results. Whether transforming processes, integrating systems post-acquisition, or creating engaging dashboards, my goal is to empower organizations to unlock their full potential through data and innovation.

Welcome to DataBrew—where insights are brewed to perfection and business transformation takes center stage. Feel free to explore my work or reach out if you'd like to collaborate!
